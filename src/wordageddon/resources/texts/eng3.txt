“Men of Gondor,” Aragorn said, his voice steady but low. “We stand not because we believe we can win, but because we must. Look not at the shadow growing in the East, but at the faces beside you — brothers, fathers, friends. Let them give you courage. This is not the end. If we fall, we fall remembered. If we prevail, the world will sing of this day. Ride now, not for victory, but for hope.” The wind stirred. The black gate opened.
